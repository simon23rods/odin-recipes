Strawberry Bellini

A refreshing drink that will surely leave you asking for more!

Perfect for the summer time, this drink will be the center of attention at your pool parties!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of strawberries, hulled and slice
  • one fourth cup of confectioners' sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of brandy
  • one and a half cups of chilled sparkling wine
  • 3 large strawberries

Directions

Blend 3 cups strawberries, confectioners' sugar, and brandy together in a blender until smooth. Chill in refrigerator for 10 minutes.

Divide the strawberry mixture among 3 champagne flutes; top each with about 1/2 cup sparkling wine; stir. Garnish each glass with 1 strawberry.
